Our Story

Founded in 2022 by two friends in a small Johannesburg garage, Mzansi Brew Co. was born from a shred passion for craft beer and South African flavours.

Today we proudly brew bold, unique beers using locally sourced ingredients. Our taproom in Melville has quickly become a favourite gathering spot for beer lovers, friends and families who appreciate great taste and good vibes.
